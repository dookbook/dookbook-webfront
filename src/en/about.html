    What's Dookbook?

    Update Date: 2018-10-24

    Dookbook, Developers' Cookbook.


    Introduction

    Dookbook (Developers' cookbook) is a new generatipn knowlege engine for developers, and offers a wealth of useful code examples and snippets for all developers. It now includes over 200 recipes that range from programming language, such as Python, Web front technology (HTML, CSS, JavaScript), Java, SQL, Go, PHP, and so on, to IT foundation and frameworks, such as TCP/IP networking, Linux, nginx, redis, MySQL, jQuery, Django/Flask, and so on.

    Features

    1. Precise Search

    Totally different from traditional searching engine just like Google, Baidu, or others that throws a lengthy list of results to you to find what you want from sea of links by yourself, DookbookTM never makes itself a trouble maker. It is a knowledge engine that introduces a new algorithm and make the result offered always unique, and it is always luck (to hit your needs).

    2. Stake in Knowledge

    You could get the corresponding income according to the contribution rate.

    3. Understand Developers

    Dookbook understands developers. Developers (you) could contribute your solutions or improvements to the global DookbookTM community by Git and Markdown.
